Data Deletion

Forge.OS is an offline-first app. All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not have servers, accounts, or cloud storage — so there is nothing stored on our end to delete.

How to Delete Your Data

Delete All App Data

Uninstall Forge.OS from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored data including workouts, templates, routines, training plans, nutrition logs, achievements, and preferences.

  • iOS: Long-press the Forge.OS icon > Remove App > Delete App
  • Android: Settings > Apps > Forge.OS > Uninstall

Delete Data Without Uninstalling

Open the App, go to Settings > Wipe All Data. This permanently deletes all workout data, templates, routines, and settings while keeping the App installed.

Export Before Deleting

If you want to keep a copy of your data before deleting, use the App's export features:

  • Settings > Export All Data — saves a .forge backup file
  • QR sharing — export individual templates and routines

Subscription Data

If you have an active Forge Pro subscription, deleting the App does not cancel your subscription. Cancel your subscription first:

  • iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Forge.OS
  • Android: Google Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Forge.OS

RevenueCat Purchase History

Forge.OS uses RevenueCat for subscription management. RevenueCat stores an anonymous purchase record (not linked to your name or email). If you want this anonymous record deleted, email us at dev@heavyc.dev and we will submit a deletion request to RevenueCat on your behalf.

What We Don't Store

Since Forge.OS does not collect or store any personal data on servers, there is no account to delete, no profile to remove, and no cloud data to erase. Your data exists only on your device, under your control.
