Who We Are

Just a dev scratching his own itch.

Heavy C Development Studio, LLC is a one-person indie software studio. There's no team of product managers, no committee decisions, no endless sprint cycles — just a single developer who builds apps to solve problems that come up in real life.

The idea is simple: if something is frustrating or missing, build the tool that fixes it. And if that tool might help someone else too, ship it and share it.

Every app that comes out of this studio started as something built for personal use. That means the features are practical, the UX is direct, and there's no filler — just what's actually useful.

What We Believe

How apps should work.

Your data is yours

Apps shouldn't require accounts to do basic things. Your information lives on your device and stays there unless you say otherwise.

Offline by default

A good app works whether you have Wi-Fi or not. Internet should be a feature, not a requirement.

No bloat

Features that aren't useful don't ship. Simple, focused apps beat overloaded ones every time.

Built to last

Apps are designed to be maintained and improved over time — not abandoned after launch.
