Frequently Asked Questions

General
Is Forge.OS free?

Forge.OS has a free tier with core workout tracking features. Forge Pro unlocks the full experience including advanced analytics, all themes, achievements, periodization, and more. A 24-hour full-access preview is included on first install, followed by a 1-month free trial of Pro.

Does Forge.OS require an internet connection?

No. Forge.OS is 100% offline. All data is stored on your device and all features work without internet. The only network activity is subscription verification through the App Store / Play Store.

Does Forge.OS collect my data?

No. We don't have servers, don't require accounts, and don't use analytics SDKs. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Data & Backup
How do I back up my data?

Use the QR sharing or .Forge file export features to export your templates, routines, and training plans. We recommend exporting any time you want a back up since the data is only stored on your device.

What happens if I delete the app?

All your data is permanently deleted. Since we don't store your data on any server, it cannot be recovered after deletion. Export your data before uninstalling.

Can I transfer my data to a new phone?

If you're transferring to a new device using your platform's backup/restore (iCloud backup for iOS, Google backup for Android), your app data should transfer with it. You can also export and re-import your templates and routines via QR codes or .Forge files.

Subscriptions
How do I manage my subscription?

iOS: Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Forge.OS

Android: Google Play Store > Profile > Payments & subscriptions > Subscriptions > Forge.OS

How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel through your device's subscription settings (see above). Canceling stops future charges but you keep Pro access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open Forge.OS, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." This works across devices signed into the same Apple ID or Google account.

I was charged but don't have Pro access.

Try restoring purchases first (Settings > Restore Purchases). If that doesn't work, email us at support@heavyc.dev with your device type and approximate purchase date.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple or Google:

  • iOS: Request a refund from Apple
  • Android: Request a refund from Google Play
Workouts
Can I edit a completed workout?

Yes, completed workouts can be edited by clicking Previous Workouts and opening the workout you would like to edit.

My workout data seems wrong. What should I do?

The App's analytics and calculations are based on the data you enter. Double-check that your weight units (lbs/kg) are set correctly in Settings, and verify that your set data was entered accurately.

What is a PR Anthem?

When you hit a personal record during a workout, Forge.OS can capture the song currently playing on your device. This is stored locally and can be displayed on shareable PR cards. Music access is optional and can be denied without affecting other features.

Nutrition
How accurate is the nutrition scanner?

The scanner uses on-device OCR to read nutrition labels. It works best with clear, well-lit photos of standard nutrition fact labels. Always verify the scanned values against the actual label before logging.

How is my TDEE calculated?

The TDEE calculator uses standard formulas based on your weight, height, age, biological sex, and activity level. These are estimates — individual metabolism varies. Consult a dietitian for personalized nutrition guidance.
